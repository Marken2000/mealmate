Getting Started

MealMate is designed to be simple and efficient. Use the steps below to understand the main workflow. This page serves as a quick-start guide for the prototype.

Step-by-step

  1. Create an account and sign in (email + password).
  2. Set preferences (diet goals, allergies, budget range).
  3. Build your plan by selecting meals for the week.
  4. Generate the grocery list automatically from ingredients.
  5. Shop and cook, then mark items/meals complete.
  6. Review insights and adjust next week’s plan.

Safety & privacy

  • MealMate is not a substitute for medical or nutritional advice.
  • Use strong passwords and log out on shared devices.
  • Avoid entering sensitive financial information.

Troubleshooting

Can’t log in? Check email/password and internet connection.
List not updating? Refresh or re-open the app.
Missing items? Verify the meal ingredients were saved.
