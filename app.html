MealMate Application Overview

MealMate is a smart meal planning application concept that combines meal scheduling, grocery list automation, and nutrition awareness in one workflow. The goal is to reduce time spent planning, minimize food waste, and help users make informed choices.

Core features

  • Meal Planner: build weekly plans using saved meals and preferences.
  • Auto Grocery List: ingredients compile into one list by category.
  • Nutrition Signals: quick view of calories/macros to support goals.
  • Cloud Sync Ready: access plans across devices (future expansion).
